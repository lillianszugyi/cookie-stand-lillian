Logo

The salmon symbol, logotype, and tagline appear on almost every piece of communication. It is prominent in the header of the website.

ALTERNATE: When it's not possible to use the salmon symbol, use just the logotype and tagline.

Colors

Colors are based on the salmon symbol. Be consistent in using the colors in this guideline.

Background color is usually beige. Accent colors are orange and dark orange. Supporting colors are off-white and black. They can be used for content and are intentionally neutral to give focus to the content.

Typography

Font families can be found on Google Fonts. 'Walter Turncoat' is used for the logotype and all major headings, in Dark Orange. 'Karma' is a serif font meant for body copy in Black. 'Cutiv Mono' is a monospace font for lists and data also in Black.
